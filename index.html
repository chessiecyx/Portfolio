I am a dual-degree student at Duke University and Duke Kunshan University studying Computation and Design. My work moves across interactive products, games, and artistic systems, with an emphasis on emotional interaction, playful structures, narrative framing, and expressive technical execution. 我目前就读于杜克大学与昆山杜克大学联合培养项目，主修 Computation and Design。 我的创作横跨交互产品、游戏和艺术系统，关注情感交互、 playful 的结构设计、叙事表达，以及具有表现力的技术实现。
