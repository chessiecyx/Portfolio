Overview 简介

About the Game 关于这个游戏

DRAMAAAA! is a social board game themed around the entertainment industry. Players build status through money, trend, and reputation, while also dealing with favors, reversals, and scandal. DRAMAAAA! 是一个以娱乐圈为主题的社交型桌游。玩家通过金钱、热度和声望建立自己的地位，同时也要应对人情交换、局势反转与丑闻曝光。

The overall tone is theatrical, dramatic, and playful. Rather than focusing on realism, the game turns celebrity culture into a competitive social system full of timing, tension, and performance. 整体气质偏戏剧化、夸张且富有玩味。这个游戏并不追求写实，而是把“明星文化”转化为一个充满时机、张力与表演性的竞争性社交系统。
